WhiteNoise O'rnatish


1) Birinchi Qadam:

PowerShell-ni Ochib env(Muhit) ni aktivatsiya qilamiz:

pip install whitenoise

2) Ikkinchi Qadam:

1) Proyektimmizdagi setting.py ni ochamiz va "MIDDLEWARE" qismiga o'tamiz

2) "django.middleware.security.SecurityMiddleware", - Shuni takiga buni yozamiz --> "whitenoise.middleware.WhiteNoiseMiddleware",

3) Uchinchi Qadam:

1) Proyektimmizdagi setting.py ni ochamiz va "INSTALLED_APPS" qismiga o'tamiz

2) "django.contrib.messages", - Shuni takiga buni yozamiz --> "whitenoise.runserver_nostatic",

4) Uchinchi Qadam(So'ngi):

PowerShell-ni Ochib env(Muhit) ni aktivatsiya qilamiz:

pip freeze > requirements.txt
